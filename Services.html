Indicators

This Thermostat is also built-in with some special indicators so that it lets you know the temperature status of your room. For details about the different types of indicators it has to offer, please scroll down for more info.

Different type of Indicators

When the room is too Cold

Sad Face

If you want to know if the temperature of your room is too Cold, Then take a look at the thermostat and see if it represents a sad face from the led lights. If it does show a sad face then the temperature of the room is too cold from the recommended temperature settings. And the thermostat will turn on immediately so that it will start heating up the room for you, once it is on of course. It will start heating up that room for 30 minutes and after those 30 minutes, If it is still below the threshold, it will remain on for 30 more minutes.

When the room is in perfect Temperature

Smiley Face

If You Want To Know If The Temperature Of Your Room Is Perfect, Then Take a Look at The Thermostat and See if it represents a Happy Face From The Led Lights. If It Does Show A Happy Face Then The Temperature Of The Room Is Perfect From The Recommended Temperature Settings. And The Thermostat Will remain on since the temperature of the room isn't too hot yet.It Will remain Heating Up That Room For 30 Minutes And After Those 30 Minutes, If It Is Still at perfect temperature, It would then still be on but at very low constant heat.

When the room is too Hot

Angry Face

If you feel that the tempearute of the room is too Hot, Then take a look at the thermostat and see if it represents an angry face from the led lights. If it does show an angry face then the temperature of the room is too warm from the recommended settings. And the thermostat will turn off Immediately so that the temperature of the room can get cooler. If it is still on off course. And after the thermostat has been off for 30 minutes, if the temperarture is still above the threshold then the thermostat will remain off.
